About Us

Healing Through Excellence

Maadhuri College of Physiotherapy was established in 2023 with a vision to create world-class physiotherapy professionals who combine clinical expertise with compassionate care.

Located in the holy city of Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, our institution provides quality education and hands-on clinical training that prepares students for rewarding healthcare careers.

🎯
Our Mission

Comprehensive physiotherapy education combining theory with hands-on clinical practice.

🌟
Our Vision

To be the leading physiotherapy institution in Andhra Pradesh, known for academic excellence.

💡
Our Values

Integrity, compassion, innovation and excellence in education and patient care.

B.P.T — 4 Years + 6 Months Internship
